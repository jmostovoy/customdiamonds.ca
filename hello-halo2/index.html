"Heavenly Halos"

A very unique custom job. Our goal was to transform an old engagement ring into something beautiful & modern.

Our client had a sentimental wedding band from her mother that she wanted to start wearing with her engagement ring - the problem however was that these two rings wouldn't fit side-by-side. She also wanted to go from a single, vintage-style halo, to a modern double halo ring. With a good amount of design work, we re-worked every single piece of her engagement ring into this new beautiful ring which sat flush with her mother's ring!

If you like this ring or would like to incorporate certain elements into your dream design, please feel free to contact us or email: info@customdiamonds.ca!

Specifications: Centre - 0.9ct natural cushion diamond, Inner Halo - 20x 1.1mm natural round diamonds, Outer Halo - 24x 1.3mm natural round diamonds,12x 2mm & 6x 2.2mm natural round diamonds along the shank. All in 18K white gold. Total: 63 natural diamonds!
