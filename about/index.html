About Us

Hello & Welcome to Custom Diamonds Canada!

We are a boutique family-run Jeweler located in Thornhill, Canada. We specialize exclusively on custom/bespoke Jewelry that is 100% designed and manufactured here in Canada! Our promise to all our customers is exceptional service at "rock" bottom prices. We have a wealth of experience manufacturing all types of jewerly: from engagment rings, wedding bands, earrings, to pendants & necklaces.

What our Design Process is like:

  1. First, please contact us with your ideas via either our email or our Contact Us Form! Being as descriptive as possible with your project will help us narrow down on an estimated quote as quickly as possible.
  2. If approximate pricing works for you, we'll continue to narrow in on design, timeliness, and diamond or gemstone requirments.
  3. At this stage, we have the option to jump on a call (video or cell) to discuss exact specifics for your project. If you're located in Toronto, we can alternatively meet in person. If you'd like to proceed, we'll order in any necessary stones upon reciept of a $250 non-refundable deposit. We'll be happy to explain at detail every single step involved in the process and all the decisions that will need to be made by yourself.
  4. Once the Diamond or Gemstone arrives, either we can meet once more so that you may look at the stone yourself, or I can pass the stone directly along to my designer to begin the 3D CAD design process. To begin design & manufacturing work, we ask for a deposit of 50%.
  5. Approximately 3 weeks later given a design of average-complexity, your Jewelry should be complete. We have the option to have your Jewelry professional appraised as well should you desire. Remaining balance will be due upon local delivery or prior to shipping.

How we Began:

Back in 2023, Jonathan, a PhD in Mathematics, had the very difficult task of purchasing an engagement ring for his soon-to-be fiancé, and later wife, Shoshana. Problem was: professionally Shoshana is an artist: so the self-imposed design standard wasn't to be trifled with. To design a unique, 

of making my own ring (I recently got married), I made connections with lots of people in the Jewelry industry here in Toronto & New York, and understand exactly how the process works from sourcing stones to 3D CAD design to wax molding & setting stones. Unfortunately, the industry is incredibly tight-lipped about true pricing, and most retailers like Blue Nile & Brilliant Earth are charging markups of 500-1000%+! I aim for around a 25% markup in comparison.

100% everything would be custom for you. If you have any quotes from other Jewellers, I promise to beat absolutely everyone else on price. If you decide to buy your ring with me, I'll be more than happy to help walk you through the process and explain all the decisions that need to be made along the way!
