About Us

Hello & Welcome to Custom Diamonds Canada!

We are a boutique family-run Jeweller located in Thornhill, Canada. We specialize exclusively on custom/bespoke Jewelry that is 100% designed and manufactured here in Canada! Our promise to all our customers is exceptional service at "rock" bottom prices. We have a wealth of experience manufacturing all types of jewelry: from engagement rings, wedding bands, earrings, to pendants & necklaces.

What our Design Process is like:

  1. First, please contact us with your ideas via either our email or our Contact Us Form! Being as descriptive as possible with your project will help us narrow down on an estimated quote as quickly as possible.
  2. If approximate pricing works for you, we'll continue to narrow in on design, timeliness, and diamond or gemstone requirments.
  3. At this stage, we have the option to jump on a call (video or cell) to discuss exact specifics for your project. If you're located in Toronto, we can alternatively meet in person. If you'd like to proceed, we'll order in any necessary stones upon reciept of a $250 non-refundable deposit. We'll be happy to explain at detail every single step involved in the process and all the decisions that will need to be made by yourself.
  4. Once the Diamond or Gemstone arrives, either we can meet once more so that you may look at the stone yourself, or I can pass the stone directly along to my designer to begin the 3D CAD design process. To begin design & manufacturing work, we ask for a deposit of 50%.
  5. Approximately 3 weeks later given a design of average-complexity, your Jewelry should be complete. We have the option to have your Jewelry professional appraised as well should you desire. Remaining balance will be due upon local delivery or prior to shipping.

How we Began:

Back in 2023, Jonathan, a PhD in Mathematics, had the very difficult task of purchasing an engagement ring for his soon-to-be fiancé, and later wife, Shoshana. Problem was: professionally Shoshana is an artist: so the self-imposed design standard wasn't to be trifled with. To design a unique ring incorporating numerous different design elements turned out to be incredibly difficult. The few jewellers that he visited first weren't interested in the large amount of work involved, and anyone that was, the price-points were unfathomable.

The solution: become our own jeweller. Over the course of a few weeks, Jonathan developed great relationships with suppliers, casters, polishers, setters, & appraisers here in Toronto. With all this knowledge and connections made - there seemed to be a large opportunity to provide quality custom jewelry at a fraction of retail prices, so Custom Diamonds Canada was born.
