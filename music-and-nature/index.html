"Music & Nature in Harmony"

A very special engagement ring! 

The inspiration was finding a way to blend nature & music. The design processes was quite laborious: taking about 20 hours of research, and another 20 hours of CAD design work, and ~15 revisions! But, we think the results speak for themselves in how unique & beautiful this ring came out!

If you like this ring or would like to incorporate certain elements into your dream design, please feel free to contact us or email: info@customdiamonds.ca!

Specifications: 4.64ct centre diamond (round brilliant "ideal" cut, F colour, VVS2 clarity). All set in 14K yellow gold.
