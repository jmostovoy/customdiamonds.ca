"Simply Elegant"

Our goal here was to create a minimalistic and elegant ring which complimented & would showcase a gorgeous 3.63ct round brilliant diamond. We opted for a classic tiffany style ring, with 6 prongs for extra durability. This ring was designed to allow our client to comfortably wear it daily, with the peace of mind regarding the diamond's security.

If you like this ring or would like to incorporate certain elements into your dream design, please feel free to contact us or email: info@customdiamonds.ca!

Specifications: The diamond is a 3.63ct, F colour, VVS2 clarity, Ideal Cut. And the setting is an elegant 6-prong, 2.7mm solitaire in 18K white gold.
