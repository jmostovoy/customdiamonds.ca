Special Pricing for Simchas

Hello & Mazel Tov!

Simchas should be a source of pure joy, free from worry, stress, or financial burden. To support your beautiful simcha, we would love to extend the following offer: 15% off any diamond(s) purchased, AND 10% off everything else (gemstones & settings). This offer is only valid for purchases made by family or close friends who will be directly involved in the simcha; or if the jewellery purchased is a gift for a Chatan/Kallah or Bar/Bat-Mitzvah.

To claim this offer, kindly email us at info@customdiamonds.ca or text/WhatsApp Jonathan personally @ (416) 801-9755. We promise to make the process as seamless & stress-free as possible no matter your budget, timeframe, or prior knowledge about jewellery!
